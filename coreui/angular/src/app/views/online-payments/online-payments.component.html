Razorpay is an Indian payment gateway which allows your customer to pay via cards, netbanking and wallets.

Online Transaction Fees

2% fee + 18% GST on the fee, per transaction (Domestic cards, Netbanking and wallets)

3% fee + 18% GST on the fee, per transaction (International, Diners and Amex cards)
